HOW TO PREPARE SPAGHETTI

INGREDIENTS

  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Ground meat
  • Oil
  • Spices and herbs
  • Basil
DURATION: 30 MINUTES

CLICK TO SEE RECIPE

RECIPE

  • Boil the pasta

  1. Fill a large pot with 4 to 5 litters of water
  2. Bring the water to a rapide boil over high heat
  3. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of salt to the boiling water. This add flavor to the paste
  4. Drop the spaghetti into the water and stir it gently so the strand do not stick together
  5. boil for 8 to 10 minutes
  6. Pour the spaghetti into a strainer in te sink to remove the water

  • Preparing the paste

  1. pour 30ml of olive oil into a deep pan over medium heat
  2. Add finely chopped onions , garlic
  3. Stir the mixture for about 5 minutes until the onions turns translucent
  4. Add grounded meat into the pan
  5. Stir in salt and herbs . Mix in one tablespoon pf concentrated tomato paste
  6. Stir in constantly for 2 minutes to caramelize the paste and deepen the flavor

  • Toss with pasta

  1. Transfer your cooked drained spaghetti straight into the sauce pan
  2. Toss the pasta over low heat for 1 minute so it absorbs the flavors
  3. Plate the spaghetti and finish by adding fresh basil to it
